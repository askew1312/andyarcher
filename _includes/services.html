Therapy

The body can be a powerfully ally in our quest for self-knowledge. Body-based therapies are centered in the idea that we hold experiences and emotions in our bodies on both physical and mental levels. When we embody our experiences, we are connected with the fullness of our pleasures and pains, and have at our hands a powerful instrument for healing. Living in deep connection with our bodies roots us in our personal histories, and allows us to tell our stories in a way only we can.

This work empowers people to transform themselves by giving them permission to step into leadership and honor their unique gifts. I believe that claiming our power begins with finding our voice, and that by examining our experiences of injustice on a bodily level we can uproot the conditions for our own oppression. A variety of therapeutic modalities find their way into my work including Core Energetics, TRE: Tension and Trauma Release, mindfulness-based stress reduction, dialectical behavioral therapy and transpersonal psychology.

I am currently offering individual sessions in New Haven, CT as a Core Energetics practitioner-in-training.

While I do not accept insurance, I offer my services on a sliding scale basis.
A sliding scale is a tool I use to make this work accessible to people from a variety of experiences. Sliding scales are based on individual income levels, with people of higher incomes paying more. I ask that you are honest about your ability to pay, and that you don’t stress too much about the fee.
Fees range between $50 - $80 per 50 min session. I have a limited number of slots available at a lower rate. I accept cash, Venmo, PayPal and personal checks.

Consider paying more on the scale if you

travel recreationally
have access to family money and resources in times of need
work part time or intermittently by choice
own the home you live in
have investments, retirement accounts, or inherited money
have a relatively high degree of earning power due to your level of education or class background

Consider paying less if you

are supporting children or other dependents
have been denied work due to discrimination
have immigration-related expenses
are an elder with limited financial support
make under $30,000 a year (not by choice)
cannot find affordable, affirming care due to your identity
